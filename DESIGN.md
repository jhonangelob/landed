# Avionics Precision Design System

Aerospace-grade clarity for high-stakes career transitions. Minimalist, technical, and high-performance.

## Colors

### Brand Colors

- **Primary:** `#0091FF` (Avionics Blue) - Used for primary actions, active states, and brand anchors.
- **Surface:** `#f9f9fc` (Clear Skies) - The primary background color for the application.
- **Surface-Container:** `#ffffff` - Used for cards and elevated containers.
- **Outline:** `#e2e8f0` - Subtle borders for cards and input fields.

### Semantic Status (Telemetry)

- **Spotted:** `#64748b` (Slate Gray)
- **Applied:** `#3b82f6` (Horizon Blue)
- **In Flight:** `#f59e0b` (Amber Alert)
- **Interviewing:** `#8b5cf6` (Radar Purple)
- **Offer:** `#10b981` (Clearance Green)
- **Landed:** `#0091FF` (Mission Success)

## Typography

- **Headlines:** `Plus Jakarta Sans` - Bold weights (700+) for page titles and section headers.
- **Body:** `Plus Jakarta Sans` - Regular weights (400-500) for standard readability.
- **Technical Data:** `DM Mono` - Used for telemetry, counts, and status indicators to provide a "flight log" feel.
- **Secondary Accents:** `Outfit` - Semi-bold for labels and navigation items.

## Geometry & Spacing

- **Corner Radius:** `4px` (Round Four) - Applied to all cards, buttons, and input fields for a professional, precise look.
- **Spacing Rhythm:** `16px` (MD) - The standard gap for margins, padding, and layout grids.
- **Information Density:** High-density layout with tight vertical spacing and clear horizontal alignment.

## Components

- **Navigation Bar:** Fixed top bar with a glassmorphism effect, featuring the "Landed" wordmark (Structured) and search integration.
- **Status Cards:** White containers with 1px border, 4px rounding, and clear typographic hierarchy.
- **Input Fields:** Soft borders with subtle gray outlines and 4px rounding.
